All bill payments at one place

Simply connect all your utility and credit card bill providers in one place. No logging in to different websites to pay bills. Once you connect your utility providers with BillzHub we enable you to pay bills, make credit card payments, set reminders, track your expenses and lots of other cool stuff.

We maximize your credit card rewards

With our smart payment choice feature we intelligently select a credit card for bill payment that will maximize your reward points. So if Chase is providing 10x bonus for PGE payment, we want to make sure you get those 10x points. Don't worry, we also ensure that your credit score doesn't get impacted because of our payment.

Pay all your bills on any day of the month

BillzHub gives you the flexibillity to pay all your bills on any day of the month. We know that you prefer to hold on to your money as long as possible, so we pay for all your bills and deduct it later from your bank account or credit card.

We ensure you don't miss any payments

BillzHub always remembers when your electricity or phone bill needs to be paid. So if you are travelling, haven't checked your emails, or are simply busy with other important things in life, we make sure you or your family don't worry about defaulting on payments.

Analyze and track all your expenses

With simple and intuitive graphs, bills hub helps you track your expenses, and reward points all from BillzHub. So, if you think you are going higher on your bills or just want to manage your finances better, we help you with your decisions.
